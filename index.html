Welcome

About Songbird Psychotherapy

Songbird Psychotherapy is solely owned and operated by Caroline Biondo (that's me!). I am a nationally certified and licensed professional counselor, and I have 10+ years of experience treating eating disorders, mood and anxiety disorders, adjustment disorders, and neurodivergent individuals across the state of Tennessee.

Why "songbird" psychotherapy? So glad you asked!
If there's one thing you should know about songbirds, it's that they are far from the delicate, fragile things that you see plastered on most therapy websites. While each species of songbird is unique in its own way, they share important commonalities: they are bold; they are resilient; they are LOUD, insistent, and tenacious; and they are, to me, the embodiment of audacious joy. To the untrained eyes and ears, it's easy to miss the thousands of songs and colors that surround us, but when you learn to tune in, you'll notice the millions of tiny wonders that fill our skies and, by extension, our every day lives. It's THIS imagery that captures the spirit of my therapeutic practice. Healing is not just about finding peace, it's about cultivating unapologetic, raucous, radiant joy.
It exists all around us; why not within us, too?

My philosophy

My approach

My work is rooted in the belief that you deserve to live a meaningful life, and that you already hold all the values and strengths you need to make it happen. My role as your therapist is to help you translate your values into healthy, workable change using cognitive flexibility, self-compassion, and mindfulness as your constant companions.

Acceptance & Commitment Therapy

With ACT as the core of my work, I integrate additional techniques from CBT, DBT, and somatic theories to create a balanced therapeutic experience tailored to you.

Health at Every Size & Intuitive Eating

As an eating disorder specialist, I am a proud HAES and weight-inclusive clinician, and I weave principles of Intuitive Eating into our work together.

Affirming for all brains & bodies

As a neurodivergent therapist and a proud ally of the LGBTQIA+ community, I work to keep this practice a safe, affirming space for everyone.

Values-driven & strengths-based

Life is about more than just relief from symptoms. Together, we build the skills and the courage to move you towards what matters most to you — a life full of authenticity and joy.
